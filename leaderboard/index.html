Catch UAVs that Want to Spy You:

Detection and Tracking of Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) in the Wild and the 1st Anti-UAV Challenge


Metrics

We use two human-centric metrics for multi-human parsing evaluation, which are initially reported by the MHP v1.0 paper. The two metrics are Average Precision based on part (APp) (%) and Percentage of Correctly parsed semantic Parts (PCP) (%).


Ranking

All teams with successful submissions have a placeholder in the leaderboard, and the results of all teams will be released on 10 June. The winner of the challenge is the team with maximal numbers of top-1 ranking among all the five metrics.
