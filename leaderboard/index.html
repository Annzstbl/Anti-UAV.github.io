Catch UAVs that Watch You:

Detection and Tracking of Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) in the Wild and the 1st Anti-UAV Challenge


Metrics

Example1:We use four metrics from common semantic segmentation and scene parsing evaluations that are variations on pixel accuracy and region intersection over union (IU). The four metrics are Pixel accuracy(%) , Mean accuracy(%), Mean IoU(%) and Frequency weighted IoU(%).

Example2: Based on two basic weakly-correlated measures are chosen:

  • Accuracy:Overlap between the ground-truth and predicted by a tracker.
  • Robustness:Counts the number of times the tracker failed and had to be reinitialized.

1. Rand trackers for accuracy and robustness separately on each attribute subset.

2. Average ranking over the attributes.

3. Giving equal weight to each performance measure we average the two corresponding rankings.


Ranking

All teams with successful submissions have a placeholder in the leaderboard, and the results of all teams will be released on May 8th 2020. The winner of the challenge is the team with maximal numbers of top-1 ranking among all the five metrics.
